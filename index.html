Discover City of San Fernando

Explore the city, taste local cuisine, attend cultural events. Experience the vibrant community of City of San Fernando, Pampanga.

Top three activities to do at City of San Fernando

A Spectacular Light Show

The Giant Lantern Festival is an annual event held in San Fernando, Pampanga that showcases the impressive lantern displays made by local communities. It's a unique and memorable experience for visitors.

Experience Local Culture

Take a stroll through the city center and experience the local culture, from street food stalls to historical landmarks, there is something for everyone in San Fernando's downtown area.

Taste local cuisine - A Foodie's Paradise

San Fernando is known as the "Culinary Capital of the Philippines" and offers a variety of traditional and contemporary dishes to try.
